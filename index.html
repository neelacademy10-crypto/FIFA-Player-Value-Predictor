Key Insights
  • 01 Potential outweighs current ability. The model weights Potential higher than Overall Rating — clubs pay for ceiling, not just current form. A 22-year-old rated 75 with 90 potential is worth far more than a 32-year-old rated 78.
  • 02 Wage is a strong market signal. Wage_EUR and Value_EUR show 0.90 correlation — clubs that pay more believe the player is worth more. Wage is actually a market benchmark, not just a cost.
  • 03 Random Forest explains 72% of variance. By combining 100 decision trees, the model captures non-linear interactions between age, rating, and potential that a simple regression misses entirely.
